**Conventions.**
- File naming: `figures/<short_descriptive_name>.{pdf,png,svg}` — no spaces preferred; `grffile` allows them if needed.
- All figures introduced in prose with `\cref{fig:label}` before they appear.
- Captions argue, not describe — see `working-notes/01-conventions.tex`.
